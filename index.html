About

An inspiring story of hope and resilience, Mrs Ruth Onoja began her journey in gospel music after a personal tragedy. As an Igala woman and deaconess at Living Faith Church, she uses her platform to uplift others and serve the less privileged.

"Music is not just a melody but a message of hope and healing. My songs are a testimony to God’s faithfulness in the storm."
